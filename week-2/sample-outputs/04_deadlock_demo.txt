Sample output of: ./deadlock_demo
=================================

This program deadlocks deliberately. The output below was captured
with a 4-second timeout; the program would otherwise run forever.
Both threads acquire their first lock, then block forever trying to
acquire the second.

Starting deadlock demo. Press Ctrl+C when you've seen enough.

[A] trying to lock X
[A] got X, now sleeping briefly...
[B] trying to lock Y
[B] got Y, now sleeping briefly...
[B] now trying to lock X
[A] now trying to lock Y

[timed out after 4 seconds — process killed]
Notice that both [A] and [B] reach the 'trying to lock' line for
the SECOND lock, and never get past it. That is the deadlock.
