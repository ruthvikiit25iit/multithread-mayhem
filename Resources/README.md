# Additional Resources

This folder contains supplementary resources to help mentees build a deeper understanding of the concepts explored throughout the project.

Operating Systems and concurrency are broad and layered subjects, and exploring external references alongside the weekly material is strongly encouraged. The resources included here — books, documentation, articles, and guides — provide additional intuition and practical insight into systems programming, threading, synchronization, scheduling, and kernel design.

Mentees are encouraged to use these materials whenever they want to strengthen their conceptual understanding or explore topics beyond the scope of the weekly sessions.
